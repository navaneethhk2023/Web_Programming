My name is Navaneeth H Krishnan, 19 years old. I hail from Anchalumoodu in the district of Kollam, Kerala. I completed my matriculation in The Kings’ School, Kollam in the IGCSE syllabus. Afterwards, I attended the Kuriakose Elias English Medium School in Kottayam for my high school diploma in Science (Computer Science). From childhood, I have been known for being extroverted and talking to strangers freely and openly, so the opportunity to stay at a hostel for my high school was a very steep learning curve and an exciting journey for me.

I have always seen Engineering as a life’s dream, hence I wrote the prestigious VITEEE exam and received admission into the esteemed Vellore Institute of Technology to pursue Majors in Computer Science Engineering. This college has introduced me to a whole variety of culture, technology, and learning. I am able to interact with experienced faculty, meet people from all cultures, and take my dreams forward.

I am part of the RoboVITics club at VIT, which has given me a purpose in this college, and I am very interested in programming and studying web development from Sathya Ma’am. She teaches very nicely, and I'm excited to learn more about the subject from her—fingers crossed for this semester. By the end of four years, I hope to pursue further studies outside India, but I am still deciding on which universities to apply to. I am learning all the intricacies.
